Defining Features of Raptors

Raptor is derived from the Lation word "rapere" which means to seize, or capture. To do that, they need certain features to hunt and kill other animals. What makes raptors different from other birds are the following features:

  • hooked beaks with sharp edges
  • feet with sharp, curved claws or talons
  • keen eyesight
