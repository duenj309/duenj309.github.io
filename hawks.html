Hawks

Hawks, like falcons, are distinguished by their thin, tapered wings which allow them to fly fast and change directions rapidly. Because of their speed and agility, they have been popular hunting companions. Their popularity can be traced back to Ancient Egypt. The biggest difference between hawks and falcons is that hawks are considerably smaller. Below is an image of a red-tailed hawk, one of the most most popular species in North America.
