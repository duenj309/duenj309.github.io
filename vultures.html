Vultures

Vultures are easy to pick out, they are the best-looking of the bunch due to their bald heads. That's what makes them distinguished among their raptor cousins. They are also scavengers, not hunters. Vultures are classified into two groups, Old World and New World. Shown below is probably the most popular vulture, at least in this part of the world anyway, the California Condor.
